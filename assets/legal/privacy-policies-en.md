# Privacy Policy for Cigarrynos

**Last updated:** August 17, 2025

Noratek SRL ("we", "our", or "us"), a company registered in Belgium with VAT number BE0470043093, is committed to protecting the privacy of users of the Cigarrynos application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the App.

---

## 1. Age Restriction

Cigarrynos is intended for users who are at least 21 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 21. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from anyone under 18, we will take steps to delete that information.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you:

- Create an account  
- Purchase or manage a subscription  
- Use features of the App  
- Communicate with us directly  

### Personal Information
- Email address  
- Password (encrypted)  
- Display name (optional)  
- Profile photo (optional)  
- User-generated content related to cigars  
- Photos you upload  
- Social media handles (optional)  

### Subscription and Payment Information
- Subscription status (free/premium)  
- Subscription type (monthly/annual)  
- Purchase history  
- Transaction IDs from Google Play or RevenueCat Web Billing  
- **Note**: We do **not** collect or store credit card numbers, bank account details, or other payment method information. All payment processing is handled by Google Play Store and our subscription management partner RevenueCat (for both Android and Web App purchases).  

### Usage Data
- App usage analytics  
- Feature usage statistics  
- Device information (type, OS version)  
- IP address (for regional compliance)  
- Crash reports and performance data

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:  

- Provide, maintain, and improve our App  
- Create and manage your account  
- Process and manage your subscription (via Google Play or Web Billing with RevenueCat)  
- Verify subscription status and provide premium features  
- Respond to your comments, questions, and requests  
- Send you technical notices, updates, and support messages  
- Send subscription-related communications (renewal reminders, payment confirmations)  
- Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities  
- Prevent fraud and protect the rights and property of Noratek SRL and others  
- Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service  

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

We use Google Firebase for data storage and Google Cloud Storage for photo storage. All servers are located in Europe.  

**Security measures include**:  
- Encrypted passwords  
- HTTPS secure connections  
- Regular audits and updates  
- Restricted data access on a need-to-know basis  
- Certified third-party payment processing (Google, RevenueCat)  

---

## 5. Sharing of Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in these cases:

### Service Providers
- **Google Firebase**: Data storage and authentication  
- **Google Cloud Storage**: File and photo storage  
- **Firebase Cloud Messaging**: Push notifications  
- **RevenueCat**: Subscription management and analytics (Android & Web App)  
- **Google LLC**: Android app distribution and payment processing  
- **Sentry**: Crash reporting and error tracking  

### Legal Requirements
- If required by law or authorities  
- To protect rights, safety, or property  
- To enforce Terms of Service  

### Business Transfers
- If we undergo merger, acquisition, or sale

## 6. GDPR Rights

As an EU resident, you have rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, and portability. Requests can be made via **privacy@cigarrynos.app**. We respond within one month.

## 7. Subscription Data Management

- We store subscription confirmations, status, and entitlements.  
- Data retention: active while subscription is ongoing; 7 years for legal/tax compliance after cancellation.  
- Users can view subscription status in-app, export history, or request deletion (subject to legal requirements).
